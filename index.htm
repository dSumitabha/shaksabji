Preface

Given the covid crisis it has become an absolute necessity for us to stay in our homes to keep ourselves safe and to stop the spread of this corona virus.

But still people have to go outside of their homes to buy some essential commodities like groceries, vegetables, medicines etc. Because of that in places like some sabji markets we can observe that there are gatherings which can be very dangerous for us and for our society as well. In view of that we have taken a small initiative to tackle this problem.

We have developed this website from which any vegetables can be ordered online and will be delivered in your home within some short span of time. We can assure you that this small initiative can result in a very significant impact on our society in this challenging time.

It is our earnest request to you, please stay in your homes to make yourself, the society and in a large scale the country safe.
